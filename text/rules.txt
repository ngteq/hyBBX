HyBBX — Legal notice and acceptable use

System description
HyBBX is a multi-transport session daemon for bandwidth-constrained networks.
It binds TCP telnet, AX.25 packet radio, and HBX/TCP circuit adapters to one
session core. Secondary instances bridge RF or limited-throughput uplinks to
a Main HBX hub. Sessions use a text-only traffic profile (default 2400 baud
8N1 pacing). Commands: /help.
