HyBBX — Terms of use

HyBBX provides sessions over several transports (telnet, SSH, WebSocket).
Packet-radio links exist in software; on-air use is not yet field-proven.

1. Your responsibility
Access is at your own risk. You answer for what you send, post, and do.
The operator is not responsible for how users behave.

2. Identity
Real names are not required. A nickname or callsign is enough. Account
details used for administration may be seen by Sysop/Admin staff only.

3. Data
User data is not sold. Mail and accounts remain on this host unless
disclosure is required by law.

4. Logging
Sessions and security-related events may be recorded (for example in
security.log) to run the service, fix faults, and deter abuse.

5. Acceptable use
Do not break the law, harass others, or try to damage the system or its
users. Staff may limit or revoke access.

6. Changes
The operator may revise this notice in text/rules.txt. Read the current
text anytime with /rules or /legal.
